## FAQ

**1. Does this require linking my corporate credit card or AWS account?**  
No integrations or permissions required. You simply input your current seat counts and monthly spend manually. We analyze the numbers locally in your browser.

**2. How do you calculate the savings? Are you using AI?**  
We don't trust AI to do financial math. Our audit engine runs on deterministic, hardcoded logic based on the latest verified pricing tiers from official vendors. We only use AI to write the personalized text summary of your results.

**3. Is this tool really free? What's the catch?**  
Yes, it's 100% free. SignalSpend is built by Credex. If your audit reveals massive savings opportunities (>$500/mo), we will offer you the option to book a consultation to buy discounted AI infrastructure credits directly through us. If not, enjoy the free advice!

**4. Which AI tools do you currently support?**  
We currently audit seat-based and API-based pricing for Cursor, GitHub Copilot, ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Windsurf, OpenAI API, and Anthropic API. 

**5. Is my company's tech stack data kept private?**  
Absolutely. Your audit runs entirely in your browser. We do not save your data to our servers unless you explicitly submit your email for a consultation or click "Share audit." Even then, public share links are strictly anonymized and stripped of all PII.
