# Unit Economics & Mathematical Viability

If Credex deploys the SignalSpend audit tool as a lead-generation asset, the viability of the project depends entirely on the conversion funnel and the Lifetime Value (LTV) of the captured leads.

## The Value of a Converted Lead (LTV)
When a startup is identified as having >$500/mo in AI savings, Credex steps in to sell them discounted AI credits. 
- **Assumptions:** The average closed lead spends $2,500/month on AI tooling. Credex provides a 25% discount, reducing their bill to $1,875/month. Credex takes a 15% margin on the backend volume.
- **Gross Profit per Month:** $281.25
- **Lifespan:** The average Series A/B startup retains their core engineering stack for 24 months.
- **LTV per closed customer:** $281.25 × 24 = **$6,750**.

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) by Channel
Based on the GTM plan, we rely entirely on zero-paid-budget strategies, making our CAC extremely low, driven only by time/labor costs.
- **Hacker News / Reddit (Organic Content):** 
  - Time spent writing: 4 hours (~$200 labor). 
  - Generates ~400 unique visitors. 
  - CAC = **$0.50 per visitor**.
- **Cold Outbound to Fractional CFOs:** 
  - Time spent prospecting + DMing: 10 hours (~$500 labor). 
  - Yields 5 active CFO partners who run 10 clients each through the tool. 
  - CAC = **$10.00 per audit completed**.
- **Credex Dead-Lead Reactivation (Email Blast):** 
  - Time to write/send: 1 hour (~$50 labor).
  - Reactivates 100 dead leads to take the audit. 
  - CAC = **$0.50 per audit completed**.

## The Conversion Funnel to Profitability
To ensure this tool is highly profitable given the labor costs, the funnel must perform at the following baseline metrics:
1. **Landing Page to Audit Completed:** 15% (Users are highly motivated by the promise of instant math).
2. **Audit Completed to Credex Lead Submitted:** 10% (Only high-spend users >$500/mo see the prominent Credex pitch; of those, we assume a conservative opt-in).
3. **Lead Submitted to Consultation Booked:** 40% (Handled via Resend automated sequences and SDR follow-up).
4. **Consultation to Credit Purchase (Closed Won):** 25% (A high close rate because the savings are mathematically proven on the call).

**Total Funnel Conversion:** `0.15 × 0.10 × 0.40 × 0.25 = 0.0015` (or **0.15%** of all unique visitors become paying Credex customers).

## Driving $1M ARR in 18 Months
To generate $1,000,000 in Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) strictly from leads sourced by this tool:
- **Required Active Customers:** At our estimated $3,375 ARR per customer ($281.25/mo), Credex needs **296 active customers**.
- **Required Leads:** At a 10% close rate from "Lead Submitted" to "Closed Won" (0.40 × 0.25), we need **2,960 submitted leads**.
- **Required Audits:** At a 10% lead capture rate, we need **29,600 completed audits**.
- **Required Traffic:** At a 15% completion rate, we need **197,333 unique visitors** over 18 months.

**Conclusion:** 
To hit $1M ARR, the tool needs to generate ~11,000 unique visitors per month. While organic launches (HN/Reddit) will drive the initial spike, sustaining 11k visitors/month requires the tool to rank organically (SEO) for terms like "AI tool pricing calculator" or "Cursor vs Copilot cost analysis," while maintaining a viral coefficient >0.2 via the OpenGraph sharing URLs.
